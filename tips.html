Tips and Resources

Healthy Habits: Lifestyle Choices

  • Limit screen time and blue light (consider blue light glasses)
  • Drink plenty of water
  • Do not skip meals, and maintain a balanced and nutritious diet
  • Try to maintain a regular sleeping schedule, and avoid daytime naps

What To Keep With You





Pain Alleviation Tips

Massage your temples, eyebrows, or spots of pain with the tips of your fingers or knuckles

Cool down: change into a t-sleeve shirt or shorts, hold an ice pack to your head, or even eat a bowl of ice cream

Ginger: for headache-induced nausea, we recommend Ginger Chews or Ginger Tea

Drink water: dehydration worsens headaches, and some medication and vitamins have dry mouth side effects

Eat a snack when taking medication to mitigate side effects such as nausea

Take a nap if your medication causes extreme drowsiness: fighting the urge to sleep may worsen your headache

Unclench your jaw

Get some fresh air (but make sure to #MaskUp)

Take a break! Look up from your page or screen every once in a while, do something mindless…

And if you can, just stop working for a few hours! Prioritize your self-care and health
